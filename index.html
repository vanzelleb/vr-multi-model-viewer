My Models

You can login to Sketchfab to browse and import 3D models.

You can upload your own .glb files.

This page allows you to import multiple 3D models (.glb only) and resize and reposition them in VR.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Sketchfab?

Sketchfab is one of the biggest websites to search and download 3D models.

Where do downloaded models get stored?

Downloaded models are stored in your browser.

How do I import a downloaded model into the VR scene?

Find your downloaded model, and click the Show button. The model will be loaded into the VR scene.

Can I export or share my downloaded models?

Currently, models are only available in your browser's storage.

Why does the search not find a certain model?

We only show models with licenses that allow for commercial use. If you can't find a specific model, it may be due to its license restrictions. Supported licenses include:

  • CC Attribution (by)
  • CC Attribution-ShareAlike (by-sa)
  • CC Attribution-NoDerivs (by-nd)
  • CC0 Public Domain (cc0)
  • Free Standard (free-st)
  • Standard (st)
