    Frequently Asked Questions

    Why do I need to log in with Sketchfab?

    Logging in with Sketchfab allows you to search and download models that are available for download, following Sketchfab's API and licensing requirements.

    Where do downloaded models get stored?

    Downloaded models are stored in your browser's storage. All model files are saved as base64-encoded data and are only accessible from this browser and device.

    How do I import a downloaded model into the VR scene?

    Go to the My Models page, find your downloaded model, and click the Import button. The model will be loaded into the VR scene.

    Can I export or share my downloaded models?

    Currently, models are only available in your browser's storage. Export and sharing features may be added in the future.

    Why do some models fail to import?

    Some models may have complex dependencies or unsupported formats. Only models with downloadable .gltf/.glb files are supported. If a model fails to import, try another one.

    Why does the search not find a certain model?

    CombineVR only shows models with licenses that allow commercial use. Supported licenses include:

    • CC Attribution (by)
    • CC Attribution-ShareAlike (by-sa)
    • CC Attribution-NoDerivs (by-nd)
    • CC0 Public Domain (cc0)
    • Free Standard (free-st)
    • Standard (st)
    Models with non-commercial or editorial licenses are not shown in search results. If you can't find a specific model, it may be due to its license restrictions.
